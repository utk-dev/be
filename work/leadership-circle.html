Leadership

Circle

Why?

We want to disrupt the hierarchical and siloed leadership practices in organisations to a project-driven collective leadership driven by agile collaboration & distributed decision-making, creating environments to nurture entrepreneurial & leadership skills. Consequently, building thriving organisations which are innovative, fast & relevant in the age of uncertainty & ambiguity.

What?

We design team-centric leadership programs customised to unique challenges of different organisations to nurture the leadership potential who could lead decentralised mission-driven teams. Our programs impact at three levels of the organisation:

Individual

We enable individuals to embark on the journey of a growth mindset -embracing change & building grit in challenging times. In the process, they discover their leadership gifts, empathetic core & inner motivation.

Team

We create motivated, collaborative & mission-driven teams of empathetic individuals with effective communication & creative problem-solving skills, nourished by trust, a safe environment & robust feedback mechanism.

Organisation

We unlock the true potential by designing lean frameworks, strategies & culture with effective monitoring mechanisms, success metrics and robust processes, all you need to tell your inspiring story.

How?

Each of our programs is a compelling story and drama mirroring elements of human-interaction & leadership behaviours because stories are the most powerful tools to inspire behavioural change.

We apply several experiential & participative methods and scenario-based pedagogy, to fabricate the stories, powered by well-established processes of Design Thinking, Game Theory, Lean Startup, NVC etc.

Furthermore, we have designed & curated a wide range of tools & modules in the form of exercises, simulations, reflective practices, case studies etc. which help us to customise to serve different learning styles & needs.
