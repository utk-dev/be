Neenv

To The Crazy Ones!

Neenv brings together 25 aspiring youth on a 7-day residential immersive journey to nurture them to become next-generation entrepreneurial leaders. Neenv is a journey of self-exploration which will ignite the flames of entrepreneurial spirit and inspire you to take action. During the seven rigorous days, you will immerse in various experiential learning sessions designed to nourish your creative confidence & entrepreneurship muscle and engage with entrepreneurs & ethical leaders in an empathetic dialogue. It is just the beginning of the long journey together - of building an inspiring startup with the support of the thriving close-knit BE community as follows:

  • In the exploration of different entrepreneurial pathways and designing your entrepreneurship journey
  • Non-judgemental space to have deep conversations to nourish emotional & mental well-being
  • Mentorship by eminent entrepreneurs & leaders and further opportunities to learn hands-on

Our vision is to be a community which supports, nurtures & inspires its members to be an entrepreneurial leader challenging the status quo.

Details

Dates, Location & Weather
  • We will start at 9 AM on 14th June & ends at 6 PM on 20th June 2020
  • Venue yet to be finalised, but mostly it will be around Himachal or Delhi
  • Temperatures shall vary predictably between 14°C to 32°C
Travel & Accommodation
  • The organisation will take care of Food, stay and all other mandatory requirements during the program.
  • You will have to reach the venue by Day 0 night or early morning on Day 1 on your own.
Financial Cost

As a nonprofit organisation, our sincere intention is to make this valuable program accessible to everyone, irrespective of their socio-economic background. We aim to accommodate varied paying capacity of participants and at the same time support the organising team by covering the costs of the program.

The cost per participant (for 25 participants) is Rs. ​15,000. We invite you to contribute on a trust-based sliding scale of Rs. 5,000 to Rs. ​15,000 and we fundraise the deficit. Since the importance of this form of experiential learning is still building up in our country, we invite you to look at it as an investment in yourself.

We want to cultivate the spirit of care & support in our community. So if you are interested in Neenv and find it difficult to contribute, please write to us for a scholarship because we believe finance shouldn't be a roadblock in anyone's learning.

Eligibility

We embrace diversity and welcome people of all backgrounds. You don't need to have prior business experience however our we actively look for the following criteria:

  • Passionate about entrepreneurship, self-motivated & disciplined
  • Age group 18-32 years (exceptions on a case-to-case basis)
  • Willing to take actively contribute to the community
  • Program is open to both Indian and International applicants

Commitment

Neenv is a rigorous program where you have to commit at least 60-70 Learning Hrs during the 7-day residential. Like a day of a hardworking entrepreneur, we will start our day around 6 AM & end around 8 PM. We expect you to take an active part in your learning and invest time, energy, and dedication into the program. Your learning would be proportional to your investment in the 7-day journey.
