Gram

Udhyami

Gram Udhyami is a 6 Month experiential learning program to nurture passionate & self-motivated rural youth to become entrepreneurial leaders by starting an enterprise in their villages. The program provides essential support in the journey of self-discovery & startup growth through intensive 3-day residential boot camps every month, regular mentorship with the social startup ecosystem leaders & active community of peers. The 3-day residential boot camps are facilitated in the local village to make learning contextual, action-oriented and accessible to aspiring rural youth.

Over the 6-month, the program design follows the problem-solving process from identifying problems to building the business model simultaneously allowing time between the bootcamps to apply learning on the ground. Next 6- month there is active support in running the enterprise but the long-term plan is to build an alumni community to support future cohorts & nourish the culture.

Our theory of change is to nurture a 3% critical mass of rural youth who will lead the movement of grassroots entrepreneurship.

Details

Dates
  • The program will span over 6 months from July to December 2020
  • There will be Mandatory Monthly 3-Day Residential Bootcamps
Travel & Accommodation
  • Monthly bootcamps will take place in Ujjain District
  • Food, stay and all other mandatory requirements during the program will be taken care of.
  • Participants will have to reach the venue by Day 0 night or early morning on Day 1 of every Monthly Bootcamp.
Financial Cost

Successful applicants are required to pay zero tuition or accommodation costs for this Program. We invest in you with trust that you will pay it forward through positive social impact in your community and society. However, you will be responsible for your travel costs to attend the Monthly Bootcamps.

Eligibility

You don’t need any educational qualifications or business experience for our Program. We embrace diversity and welcome people of all backgrounds with the following criteria:

  • Passionate, Self-motivated & Disciplined
  • Age group 18-32 years (exceptions on a case-to-case basis)
  • Citizen of Ujjain, Indore, Dewas, Ratlam District

Commitment

Gram Udhyami is a rigorous program where you have to commit at least 50-60 Hrs per month. During the 3-Day Residentials, we will start our day around 6 AM & end around 8 PM. We expect you to take an active part in your learning and invest time, energy, and dedication into the program. We strongly believe that how much you invest in the program is a key determinant for how much your entrepreneurial Journey grows over the 6 months.
