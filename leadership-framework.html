Leadership

Framework

"The place to improve the world is first in one's own heart and head and hands."
- Robert M. Pirsig

After rigorous research, We have created an inclusive and comprehensive framework that has application in broad-ranging educational environments and at different levels of learning, to build Entrepreneurial & Leadership Competencies. There are 9 Core-Competencies which engages the strengths of Head, Heart & Hand with a triple focus on Self, Social & System. Each competency is a set of skills, capabilities, behaviour & knowledge organised as per the different levels of mastery. Learning paths start with building a connection with the self then the people around us and finally becoming aware of the interdependencies.

  • Self Awareness: Emotional Awareness, Self Assessment, Self Confidence
  • Self Management: Emotional Regulation, Self Control, Self Discipline
  • Grit: Passion, Drive for Mastery, Optimism
  • Empathy: Empathetic Listening, Building Bonds, Relationship Skills
  • Problem Solving: Design Thinking, Planning, Innovation
  • Community Building: Conflict Resolution, Team Skills, Negotiation
  • Storytelling: Inspiring Others, Influence, Public Communication
  • System Thinking: Interdependency Awareness, Leveraging Systems
  • Business Skills: Business Economics, Strategy, Service Orientation

Process

Our leadership development process is a march on the journey of discovery of inner spirit & multiple realities within & around us. In the soil of trust, we develop the ability to empathetically listen to different narratives and expand our definition of self. Zooming out, we apply human-centric design thinking & simulation-based learning to build business & problem-solving skills. The process leads to the beginning of a new phase embracing the ambiguity & failure, leaving behind the old self and becoming the leader of change. There are 5 integrated elements in the process not following any orderly sequence:

Immersion

Immersion to experience different lived reality and understand multiple perspectives encompassing single truth is a key to the expansion of our narrative of the world. It helps to build empathy, curious awareness and get inspiration for innovation.

Experimentation

We consistently focus on developing space for experimentation with ideas, thoughts, models & beliefs. There is a non-judgemental permit to fail in the curious discovery of truth and questioning the status quo.

Transformation

Our core philosophy is that the circle of inner change leads to outer change. We invite participants to reflect, leave behind old self and be the leader of change. Essence to create motivation, inspiration and influence from the deepest core of our flourishing spirits. We facilitate this crucial phase with love, care & compassion.

Mentorship

Our way of mentorship focuses on active listening & asking curious questions to support the young leaders to discover solutions through their inner voice. It also creates a comfortable space which nourishes emotional well-being and instils confidence with a growth mindset.

Community

We facilitate a thriving community which brings out the true potential of each of its unique members. A group of mentors, friends & young leaders with shared values who not only push each other but also constructively support because the path of leadership is challenging.
